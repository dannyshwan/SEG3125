Our Staff

Some great things come in small packages!

Yukari

From Osaka, Japan, Yukari is known to be gentle and careful with her practice. With 5 years of experience under her belt, she can confidently cut many different styles ranging to popular styles across Canada and the US as well as some from East Asia!

Simon

A graduate from the Toronto Fade Master Academy, Simon has been in the industry for as far as he can remember. Both a perfectionist and an experimentalist, he devotes himself to delivering the best and never fails to have customers leave with a smile!

Alessandro

The founder of Bytown Cuts and son of a Montreal barber, Alessandro's passion and confident in his practice makes him a fine barber. Having over 10 years of experience, Ales' technical ability enables him to cut with great precision; making sure his customers walk out as satisfied as possible!

Anne

An Ottawa-native and a passionate stylist, Anne is creative when it comes to her practice. Never fearing the customer's requests, she always comes up with a way to give her customers exactly what they want!
